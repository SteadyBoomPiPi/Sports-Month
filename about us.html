Who are We?

We are a bunch of XMUM students who dedicated to bring XMUM community as one.

Vision

Through sports events, we aim to bring the complex community of XMUM together as one and overcome challenges faced within this international university campus.

Mission

Provide a platform to XMUM students who wish to sharpen their event managing skills and widen their social circle as efforts to understand, engage and improve the community.
